Almost 70 years after the Georgetown experiment, MaTOS ("Machine Translation for Open Science") revisits the machine translation of whole scientific documents with the aim of facilitating and opening up access to scientific knowledge. MaTOS is supported by the French National Research Agency as part of the call "AAPG 2022 - CES 23 (Artificial Intelligence and Data Science)".
