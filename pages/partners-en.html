The ALMAnaCH team (Automatic Language Modelling and Analysis & Computational Humanities) focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP), a key area within Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Digital Humanities (DH), at the crossroads between theoretical computer science, machine learning, and linguistics.
